About ToolNova

The global directory of the best AI tools. Built for makers, by makers.

Our mission

ToolNova is on a mission to make AI tools discoverable. With thousands of AI products launching every year, finding the right tool is harder than ever. We curate, categorize, and review the world's best AI tools so you can spend less time searching and more time creating.

What you'll find

  • Trending tools — what's hot right now in the AI world
  • Top rated tools — community favorites with verified reviews
  • Free tools — powerful AI you can start using at no cost
  • 16 categories — AI Chat, Image, Video, Voice, Music, Coding, Writing, Design, Productivity, Marketing, Education, Research, Business, 3D, Avatar, and Other
  • Bilingual search — search in English or Arabic (try "مولد صور" for image generators)

How we curate

Every tool in our directory is hand-picked by our editorial team. We evaluate AI products on quality, reliability, user reviews, and innovation. We never accept payment for inclusion — our rankings are independent and community-driven.
